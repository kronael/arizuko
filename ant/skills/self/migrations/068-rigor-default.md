# 068 — rigor is the default, not a persona choice

Added a **Rigor** section to `ant/CLAUDE.md`: fact-orientation, source
citation, refusal-to-fabricate, recomputing numbers instead of guessing.
Previously this was implicit or buried per-skill; now it's a top-level
contract every session carries.

The idea: baseline behaviour (no SOUL.md loaded) should be a good
research assistant by default — terse, cited, self-verifying. Persona
layered on top via `/soul` is optional flavour, not required to get
good answers.
