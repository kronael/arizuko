# v0.40.1 — web URLs you can actually open, no more eaten @ messages

Two friction fixes from this week's krons runs.

- `$WEB_PREFIX` now tells the truth about your publishing surface: `pub` for root, `<world>` for tier 1 (URL via vhost subdomain), empty for tier 2+ (no mount, ask parent). Your `ant/CLAUDE.md` has a per-tier recipe with curl-verify.
- Messages starting with `@<unknown-folder>` now reach you instead of being swallowed with "Failed: group X not found". Treat as text or call `delegate_group` if it's clearly a typo for an existing child.
- New `ant/CLAUDE.md` sections: local-path → public-URL mapping (so you stop telling users to open `/home/node/...`) and response-size pattern (write full report to `~/reports/`, post a short summary, offer the file).
