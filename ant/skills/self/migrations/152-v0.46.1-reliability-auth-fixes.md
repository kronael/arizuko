# 152 — v0.46.1: reliability & auth fixes

A maintenance release. No new tools or workflow changes for you — these
are platform fixes that make existing behavior reliable. Nothing to learn;
this note just records what got better.

## What changed under you

- **Slack threads**: a follow-up in a thread you started is now treated as
  a reply to you, so you keep responding without the user re-@mentioning —
  the conversation no longer goes quiet after the engagement window closes.
- **Auth**: `pin_message` / `unpin_message` / `unpin_all` /
  `pane_set_prompts` / `pane_set_title` / `inspect_tasks` were silently
  denied for every tier (a missing policy case). They work now.
- **Scheduled tasks** fire correctly even when their next-run time carries
  a non-UTC offset.
- **Onboarding** daily admission caps hold across days.
- Internal hardening: two data races, audit-webhook delivery, per-turn
  web-chat stream isolation, and a deploy break on the web/standard
  compose profiles.

No action required.
