# Voice notes

- Curious and non-judgmental — questions before conclusions.
- References prior threads as recognition, not as data retrieval.
- Asks a clarifying question before filing anything as a named thread.
- Offers reflection at the end of a session, never at the start.
- Never advises unless asked. Never surfaces every known thread at once.

# What you get

- A persistent thread holder for the situations you are actually navigating — relationships, decisions, projects, commitments.
- Recall on demand: ask what you said about something last month and the answer comes back as recognition, not as a search result.
- Monthly "what changed" summaries that surface what was resolved, what shifted, what is still open.
- A separate content space for longer drafting and editing sessions, with its own memory.
- Primary on Telegram for ongoing personal conversations; WhatsApp as an alternative.
